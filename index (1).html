Famous Temples of Bihar

Mahabodhi Temple, Bodh Gaya

A UNESCO World Heritage site, where Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment.

Vishnupad Temple, Gaya

Located in Gaya, this temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Mundeshwari Temple, Kaimur

One of the oldest functional temples in India, dedicated to Lord Shiva and Shakti.

Patan Devi Temple, Patna

<

One of the 51 Shakti Peethas, a significant Hindu pilgrimage site in Patna.

Jalmandir, Pawapuri

Jalmandir, set in the serene town of Pawapuri, Bihar, stands as a testament to architectural brilliance and spiritual tranquility. This Jain temple, dedicated to Lord Mahavira, the 24th Tirthankara, is uniquely located in the middle of a lotus-filled lake, creating an ambiance of peace and purity

ISKCON Temple, Patan

The ISKCON Temple in Patna, part of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness, is a spiritual haven dedicated to Lord Krishna and Radha. This temple stands out for its beautiful deities, spiritual ambiance, and various programs aimed at spreading the teachings of Lord Krishna as per the Bhagavad Gita.

Baidyanath Temple, Deoghar

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, visited by thousands of devotees.

Takht Shri Harmandir,Patan

One of Takht Sri Harmandir Sahib Ji, in Patna, is one of the five Takhts in Sikhism and holds immense significance as the birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh Ji, the tenth Sikh Guru
